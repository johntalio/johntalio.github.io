For the Love of Shadow of the Colossus

Pick on Someone Your Own Size

Shadow of the Colossus is a video game published by Sony Computer Entertainment for the Playstation 2. It was developed by Team Ico and directed by Fumito Ueda. The game was released in North America and Japan in October 2005. It is the spiritual successor to the game Ico, created by the same development team.

Shadow of the Colossus features a deceptively simple story with an incredible amount of depth and attention to detail, which has earned the game international critical acclaim, a tremendous and devoted fanbase, and a peculiar transcendent quality: today, Shadow of the Colossus is often referenced in debates regarding the art quality and emotional perspectives of video games. In the eyes of its fans around the world, it has become the standard by which other games of its ilk are judged. It is truly a defining work of art.

About Shadow

Throughout the game, little information is given to the player concerning the backstories of the characters and their relationships with one another. The game takes place in a fantasy setting, with most of the game's events occurring within a vast, unpopulated region, known as the Forbidden Land. Aptly, the Forbidden Land is physically isolated from the outside world by such geographical features as mountain ranges and oceans; the only things that inhabit the Forbidden Land are ancient ruins, crumbling structures, and, of course, the powerful colossi.

The Characters

The protagonist of the game is Wander, although he is never mentioned by name throughout the entire course of the game. He has traveled to the Forbidden Land to kill sixteen colossi in order to resurrect Mono, a maiden who was somehow sacrificed because she was believed to have a cursed destiny. Guided by a mysterious, disembodied entity called Dormin, who is rumored to have the ability to revive the dead, Wander must search the expansive, unknown region accompanied only by his faithful horse Agro for the colossi. The player meets only a few other characters during the game.
